Christopher Duigan - South African Pianist

Christopher Duigan, a South African concert pianist, composer, recording musician and Steinway artist.

Biography

Born in 1968, Christopher started learning piano at the tender age of seven under the tutelage of Anette Kerkovius. He further studied under renowned musicians including Isabella Stengel, Lamar Crowson, Albie van Schalkwyk and Arnaldo Cohen (Duigan, C., n.d.).

For the greater part of his career, Duigan has performed 100 concerts each year for 30 years from his impressive repertoire of classical and popular favourites (Duigan, n.d.). On 28 March 2020, Duigan began performing livestream concerts from his home, starting from his iPhone and building up his studio over time. (Naidoo, M. 2022)

Duigan has attracted more than a thousand subscribers on his YouTube channel and has streamed 285 concerts to date.
